About Us

Who we are

Sachdev & Co is a family run accountancy firm whose experience within the accounting sector ranges from 2 to 40 years, every member of staff is trained to the highest possible professional standard and we are always ready to assist you, with fast and friendly service. We have a client base that is diverse and is located throughout the UK. As such our staff are adaptable and always ready to accommodate your financial management needs.

As a firm of Chartered Accountants, we are registered for a wide range of services, including Audit and Taxation. We are bound by the ethical guidelines of the both The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England & Wales, the Association of Accounting Technicians, and the Chartered Institute of Payroll Professionals, and accept instructions to act for you on the basis that we will act following their ethical guidelines.

For a full list of the services, we offer please go to the services menu.

Our Services

Bookkeeping & Accounting
We offer in house accounts preparation and production of statutory accounts. A member of the Sachdev & Co team will take the information provided by you and input this into one of our financial software programmes, and will then go on to produce end of year financial statements that conform with generally accepted accounting principles.
Tax Management
We currently have numerous Personal, Partnership and Corporation self-assessment tax clients, our firm, will calculate your tax liability and assist you in the preparation of your tax return, and the submission of the return to the Inland Revenue. We will also advise you of your tax liability and will remind you when it is due for payment.

Periodic Payroll processing
Using state of the art software, we will process your monthly or weekly payroll, making payslips available to staff, prepare payroll summaries and reconcile payroll payments to the bank
